My name is Senay Tilahun. I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Computer Science at Northeastern University. I went to the University of Notre Dame, du Lac, for my undergraduate studies where I received a dual degree; a BS in Mechanical Engineering and a BA in Industrial Design. After graduating, I was recruited into a two-year fellowship program called Orr Fellowship, with a mission to recruit and develop the next generation of business leaders and entrepreneurs. As part of the fellowship, my main responsibility was my full-time position as a Product Manager at a publicly traded company, Angi (formerly Angie’s List). During my time as a PM, I established and managed a product growth team (a team with 5 engineers) to optimize conversion rates of business KPIs, and after three years of working closely with amazing engineers, I saw the true impact of having deep technical knowledge. I also noticed the lack of that knowledge as a weakness in my toolbox to make a transformational impact on society. I decided that I need a more fundamental understanding of computer science and my goal is that with a master’s degree I will build the skills, network, and confidence needed for a bigger impact.
